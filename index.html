Work in Progress

This projet aims to recreate WoW Armory or Diablo 3 character profiles for Diablo 2. My main inspirations are D3up, D3 EHP Calculator, as well as various WoW and D3 spreadsheets I came across in the past years. I'd like to particulary thank Aaron Cox, the developer behind D3up. He helped me immensely by responding to various questions by email. If you play Diablo 3 and like optimizing your characters, definitly check out D3up, it might replace your favorite spreadsheet/tool.
